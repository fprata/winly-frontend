If you do business in Portugal, **BASE** is the most important procurement portal you need to know. It's the official platform where all Portuguese public contracts are published and tracked.

## What Is BASE?

BASE (base.gov.pt) is Portugal's national public procurement portal, managed by the **IMPIC** (Instituto dos Mercados Públicos, do Imobiliário e da Construção). It serves two key functions:

1. **Contract publication** — all public contracts must be published on BASE
2. **Transparency** — historical data on all public spending is publicly accessible

## What You'll Find on BASE

BASE contains information on:
- **All public contracts** awarded in Portugal, regardless of value
- **Buyer information** — which entities are procuring
- **Contract values** — original and final amounts
- **Supplier details** — who won each contract
- **Contract modifications** — price changes, extensions, and amendments

## BASE vs TED: What's the Difference?

| Feature | BASE | TED |
|---------|------|-----|
| Coverage | Portugal only | All EU countries |
| Threshold | All contract values | Above EU thresholds only |
| Language | Portuguese | All EU languages |
| Data depth | Detailed (amendments, final values) | Notice-level |
| Historical data | Extensive (since 2008) | Current + archives |

**The key insight:** BASE captures contracts that are **too small for TED**. These below-threshold contracts are often less competitive and more accessible for SMEs.

## Types of Procurement Procedures in Portugal

Portuguese public procurement follows the **CCP (Código dos Contratos Públicos)**:

### Direct Award (Ajuste Direto)
- For contracts below specific thresholds
- Simplified procedure — faster, less bureaucratic
- Buyer invites one or more suppliers directly

### Prior Consultation (Consulta Prévia)
- For contracts up to €75,000 (goods/services) or €150,000 (works)
- Buyer invites at least 3 suppliers
- More competition than direct award, less than open tender

### Open Tender (Concurso Público)
- For higher-value contracts
- Published publicly, any supplier can bid
- Most formal and competitive procedure

### Restricted Tender (Concurso Limitado por Prévia Qualificação)
- Two-phase process: qualification, then bidding
- Used for complex or high-value contracts

## How to Use BASE Effectively

### 1. Search by Entity

If you know which public bodies are your potential buyers (municipalities, ministries, hospitals, universities), search for their past contracts. This reveals:
- What they buy regularly
- Typical contract values
- Which suppliers they work with
- When contracts are likely to renew

### 2. Track Your Competitors

Search for your competitors' names on BASE. You'll see:
- Which contracts they've won
- At what prices
- Which buyers they work with
- Their geographic focus

### 3. Analyze Price Patterns

BASE publishes both the **base price** (buyer's estimate) and the **contract price** (actual award value). Comparing these reveals typical discount patterns by sector.

### 4. Monitor Contract Renewals

Many public contracts are for fixed periods (1-3 years). By tracking when existing contracts expire, you can prepare bids for upcoming renewals.

## Key Portuguese Public Buyers

The largest procurement entities in Portugal include:

- **ESPAP** — Central purchasing body for the state
- **SPMS** — Healthcare procurement
- **Municipal councils** (Câmaras Municipais) — Local government
- **Public hospitals** — Healthcare equipment and services
- **Universities** — Research and education procurement

## Challenges with BASE

While BASE is invaluable, it has limitations:

1. **Portuguese only** — no English interface
2. **Search is basic** — limited filtering compared to modern platforms
3. **Data structure** — extracting insights requires significant manual effort
4. **No alerts** — BASE doesn't offer email notifications for new contracts

## Combining BASE with AI Intelligence

Modern procurement tools can overcome BASE's limitations by:
- **Aggregating** BASE and TED data into a single searchable interface
- **Auto-translating** Portuguese tenders for international companies
- **Scoring** opportunities based on your company profile
- **Alerting** you when relevant new contracts appear
- **Analyzing** buyer patterns and competitor moves automatically

Understanding BASE is essential for doing business with the Portuguese public sector. Combined with the right intelligence tools, it becomes a powerful source of business opportunities.
